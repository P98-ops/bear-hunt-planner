How to use this planner

What it does

  • Enter your troop totals and march capacities once, and it works out the best Host Rally + up to 6 Joiner marches to send for Bear Hunt.
  • Your numbers auto-save to this browser as you type — reopen the page later and they're still here. Nothing is sent anywhere.

Getting your numbers

  • Frakinator ratio — open the Frakinator tool with your hero screenshots to get your optimal Host Infantry/Cavalry/Archers split, then enter it under "Host Ratio".
  • Joiner ratio defaults to 10% / 10% / 80% — a safe split that keeps some Infantry and Cavalry in every Joiner march instead of sending 100% of one troop type. Adjust it if you like.

Key options

  • Use Host Rally? — turn this off if you only want to send Joiner marches (all your troops become available to Joiners instead).
  • Master VALORA and boosts (Pet Bison, Marshal, City Bonus cap) all add capacity on top of your base march sizes.
  • The Host Ratio and Joiner Ratio checks should read OK — if not, your three percentages don't add to 100%.

Saving and backing up your data

  • Save your current numbers as a named preset (e.g. a player's name), then use Export Presets to download a backup — you'll be able to choose exactly which presets to include.
  • Use Import Presets to load that file back in, here or on any other browser or device.

Player Presets

Save separate setups (different players, or your own scenarios) and switch between them instantly. Stored only in this browser — nothing is shared. Export lets you pick which presets to include; Import moves them to another device or browser.
